Late last year I came across an absolute gem in Audrey's first EP and the prospect of the band producing an album matching or even surpassing that release seemed a bit of a tall order. Visible Forms manages to not disappoint. It is slow and gentle and melancholic and penetrating and leaves me breathless. It'll take sometrhing special to top this for album of the year...
