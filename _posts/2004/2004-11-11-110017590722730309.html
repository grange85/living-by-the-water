Another lovely ballboy album


Picture from here

Oh hell...they make such lovely sounds; they have such fun/sad/funny/miserable lyrics; they have song titles nearly as long as the song itself; and if they keep chucking out albums as good as the "Royal Theatre" then maybe I guess I'll have nothing to complain about.
