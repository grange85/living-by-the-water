Back in January I decided I'd start a tradition of posting about The New Year every new year - but one post a year is not good enough (even if they can only manage to release one album every three years).



But they're playing a few dates on the left side of the US and hopefully they'll work they way around the globe and hit London sometime before the end of the year - preferably with album No. 3 in the bag.



Touch and Go records have just posted video of their appearance at last year's 25th anniversary show in Chicago - a couple of songs and a bit of an interview (about how great T&G are!) (via Bradley's Almanac).
