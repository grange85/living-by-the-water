The Crack - The Ruts
My mate Paul was a huge fan of The Ruts and for a while this and Grin and Bear It were played extensively. I'd sort of forgotten about it over the years but it really is another rather splendid punk album. It finishes with an electrifying live track Human Punk. Paul then became a huge fan of Status Quo... can't explain that at all...
