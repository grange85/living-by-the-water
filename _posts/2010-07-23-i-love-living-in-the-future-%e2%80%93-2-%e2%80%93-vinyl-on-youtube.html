I love living in the future - I love that I can get music instantly delivered to my ears without leaving my seat... but I also have a fondness for vinyl not for any reasons of quality or snobbishness but more because somehow putting a needle into a groove of plastic and getting music out of it is somehow more amazing to me than getting music out of a computer.

I love that people post videos of them playing their records to YouTube and I never get tired of watching them - here are a few favourites, most of which I found in a 30 minute trawl of YouTube - there is so much vinyl joy out there!

The Champs  - Tequila

The Monkees - Porpoise Song

The Shangri-Las - Give Him a Great Big Kiss

The Special A.K.A. - Gangsters

Luna - Dear Paulina (of course!)
