On Friday nothing works better than a song that has me bouncing up and down in my seat - as if smiling and head bobbing and foot-tapping will make the weekend come a little quicker... here are six songs that I guarantee will do just that!

Stereolab make the very very best Friday music so could actually have all six slots on this list, also one track that should be on this list isn't because I already used it in this post from earlier in the week.

Stereolab - Stomach Worm (live version here)

(or Stereolab - Lo Boob Oscillator)

Electrelane - Bells

Get a cup of tea for this one as it'll take you nearly 14 minutes closer to the weekend!
Quickspace - Superplus

Tiger - Shining in The Wood

Eleventh Dream Day - Makin' Like a Rug

Finally (and of course)
Saloon - Le Weekend
