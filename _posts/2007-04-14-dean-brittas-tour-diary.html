Britta Phillips & Dean Wareham's MySpace blog has a roundup of their west coast tour...

The Getty show was full (about 450 people), but we'd been warned beforehand that there are always members who come to every show and leave after a few songs. Luckily Jack Black (Tenacious D) stayed for our set even though he was there to see the opener (his brother-in-law) Josh Haden.
